Make-Up Assignments

If you have missed a class, please read the notes for the training in the classes section and complete the corresponding make-up assignment below. You can either make a copy of the document or download the document and edit it through Word. Send the completed assignment to Jason (jason@berkeleyfreeclinic.org). While these assignments are intended as make-up assignments, you can do some of the problems to practice some of your counseling skills.
