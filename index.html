Make-Up Assignments

If you have missed any of these classes, you will need to complete the corresponding assignment and email it to Jason (jason@berkeleyfreeclinic.org) when you are finished. Please keep in mind that while make-up assignments are available, all the information may not be fully conveyed through the assignment. Note that the weekend classes are skills that can't be learned through training so they cannot be made up through assignments. Finally, although these were created as make-up assignments, they are also great practice for those who want to work on their counseling skills and can be used as such.
